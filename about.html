About

Hello and welcome! This site is a public aggregator and personal organizer for my research work and interests. I am a practitioner researcher in the field of education (PhD) contributing research to the Virtual Reality, Internet Research and Education Lab – Department of Psychology, Panteion University of Athens.

Research interests and recent research projects include: Group work in Education; Facilitating Learning Organizations; Experiential Learning; Complex Systems Pedagogy; Learning Communities & MOOCs; Digital Storytelling in Education; Digital Cultures; Networked Grounded Theory; Research Methods in Cyberspace; computational research methods in Education.

For a clearer picture of my approach to teaching take a look at the projects, at the publications and the presentations sections at this site. To have an idea about what inspires me in the fast-forward moving networked world we all live in today, take a look at my quote blog.
